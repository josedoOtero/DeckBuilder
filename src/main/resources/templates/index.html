What is Yu-Gi-Oh! TCG?

Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game is a strategic card game based on the popular franchise. Since its release, millions of players collect and compete with cards representing monsters, spells, and traps.

  • Year of creation: 1999 (adaptation of the manga/anime to TCG during the late 90s).
  • Over 10,000 cards released across several generations and expansions (approximate and growing number).
  • Competitive format with local, regional, and international tournaments.

Yu-Gi-Oh! combines story, collection, and strategy. Building a balanced deck and knowing the rules gives you an advantage in every duel.

What is DeckBuilder?

DeckBuilder is a deck management tool designed for Yu-Gi-Oh! TCG players. It allows you to create, edit, and share your decks, track statistics, and easily consult cards.

It also provides features to export decks in popular formats, compare lists, and maintain a history of changes so you can experiment without losing work.

Stay up-to-date with Yu-Gi-Oh! TCG news, discover new archetypes, and share your strategies with the community.
